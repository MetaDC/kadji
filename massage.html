Frequently Asked Questions

What is Abhayang therapy, and how does it help?

Abhyang is a personalized Ayurvedic oil massage that promotes relaxation, improves circulation, and enhances overall well-being.

How does Abhayang therapy relieve stress?

The soothing massage helps calm the nervous system, reduce tension, and create a sense of deep relaxation.

Does Abhayang therapy help with muscle relaxation?

Yes, the warm oil and gentle massage techniques help relax muscles, ease stiffness, and promote flexibility.

What are the detoxification benefits of Abhayang?

The massage stimulates the lymphatic system, aiding in the removal of toxins and promoting internal balance.
