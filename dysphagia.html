Frequently Asked Questions

What is dysphagia therapy, and how does it help?

Dysphagia therapy focuses on improving swallowing function, reducing discomfort, and ensuring safe eating for individuals with swallowing difficulties.

How does the therapy improve swallowing?

We use targeted exercises and techniques to strengthen swallowing muscles and enhance coordination for safer swallowing.

What safe eating techniques do you teach?

We provide strategies for proper positioning, pacing, and food modifications to promote safe and comfortable eating experiences.

How do you support nutritional needs?

We offer nutritional guidance to help individuals maintain a balanced diet while addressing their specific swallowing challenges.
