Frequently Asked Questions

What services do you provide for locomotor disability care?

We offer specialized support, mobility training, physical rehabilitation, and assistance with daily activities to enhance mobility.

How does mobility training help individuals?

Mobility training focuses on improving movement skills, helping individuals regain independence and confidence in their daily activities.

What kind of physical rehabilitation do you offer?

Our physical rehabilitation programs are tailored to each individual’s needs, aiming to restore strength, balance, and coordination.

Do you provide support for assistive devices?

Yes, we assist individuals in selecting and using assistive devices to enhance mobility and facilitate daily living.
