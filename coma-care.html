Frequently Asked Questions

What services do you offer for coma patients?

We provide specialized therapies aimed at enhancing responsiveness, physical therapy, and overall supportive care.

How do you help improve responsiveness?

We use cognitive and sensory therapies to stimulate brain activity and promote patient engagement.

Why is physical therapy important in coma care?

Physical therapy prevents complications from immobility and helps rebuild muscle strength and mobility.

Is there support for families?

Yes, we offer emotional support, progress updates, and guidance to help families through the rehabilitation process.
