Frequently Asked Questions

What services do you offer in occupational therapy?

We provide personalized therapy to enhance daily living skills, improve functional mobility, and support cognitive training and adaptive equipment use.

How do you help improve daily living skills?

We teach practical strategies to assist individuals in performing everyday tasks more independently and effectively.

What is functional mobility, and why is it important?

Functional mobility refers to the ability to move safely and effectively in daily life, which is essential for maintaining independence.

Do you provide support for adaptive equipment?

Yes, we assist individuals in selecting and using adaptive equipment to enhance their ability to perform daily activities.
