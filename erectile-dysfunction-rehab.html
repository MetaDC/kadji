Frequently Asked Questions

What services do you offer in the ED Rehabilitation Program?

We provide a multidisciplinary approach including medical consultations, pelvic floor therapy, fitness guidance, and nutrition counseling to improve erectile function and overall health.

How does the program help improve erectile dysfunction?

Our therapies target the root causes, offering exercises, stress management, and medical guidance to restore natural erectile function.

Why is a healthier lifestyle important for ED recovery?

A balanced lifestyle, including fitness and nutrition, enhances overall health, addressing factors like blood flow, hormonal balance, and stress that impact erectile function.

Do you provide support for partners?

Yes, we offer resources and strategies to help partners understand and support their loved ones, strengthening relationships during the recovery journey.
