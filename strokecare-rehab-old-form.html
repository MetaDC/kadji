Frequently Asked Questions

What is Advanced Stroke Care and Rehab?

It's a specialized program that helps stroke survivors recover through personalized therapies like physical, occupational, and speech therapy.

Can I receive stroke rehabilitation at home?

Yes, many services offer home-based rehab, allowing therapists to work with patients in their environment.

What therapies are included in stroke rehabilitation?

Common therapies include physical, occupational, and speech therapy. Additional treatments may focus on cognitive skills and emotional support.

How long does stroke rehabilitation typically last?

It varies by individual. Some recover in weeks, others may need months. Rehab plans are tailored to each person's progress.
