Frequently Asked Questions

What is incontinence therapy with Functional Magnetic Stimulation (FMS)?

FMS is a specialized therapy that improves bladder control and strengthens pelvic muscles through non-invasive stimulation.

How does FMS improve bladder control?

FMS stimulates the pelvic floor muscles, helping to enhance bladder function and reduce incontinence episodes.

Is the treatment non-invasive?

Yes, FMS is a non-invasive therapy, making it a safe and comfortable option for individuals experiencing incontinence.

How does this therapy enhance quality of life?

By improving bladder control and pelvic strength, FMS helps individuals regain confidence and enjoy a more active, fulfilling life.
