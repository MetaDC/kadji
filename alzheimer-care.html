Frequently Asked Questions

What services do you provide for Alzheimer’s and dementia care?

We offer tailored strategies for memory care, daily living assistance, and safety monitoring to support individuals with cognitive challenges.

How do you enhance cognitive function in patients?

We use memory care strategies that include cognitive exercises and engaging activities to stimulate brain function.

What daily living assistance do you offer?

We help individuals with daily tasks such as bathing, dressing, and meal preparation to promote independence and comfort.

Do you provide support for families?

Yes, we offer family support services to help families navigate the challenges of caregiving and provide emotional assistance.
