Frequently Asked Questions

What services do you offer in special physiotherapy?

We provide customized physiotherapy for sports injury rehabilitation, neurological therapy, post-surgical recovery, and gait & balance training.

How do you help with sports injury recovery?

We offer targeted therapies to speed up healing, restore function, and prevent further injury in athletes.

What is neurological therapy in physiotherapy?

Neurological therapy focuses on improving movement and function for individuals with conditions like stroke or Parkinson’s disease.

Why is gait and balance training important?

Gait and balance training helps improve stability, reduces the risk of falls, and promotes confidence in walking.
