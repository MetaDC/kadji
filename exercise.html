Frequently Asked Questions

What services do you offer in exercise therapy?

We provide personalized exercise programs focused on strength building, flexibility training, cardiovascular health, and injury prevention.

How does exercise therapy help with strength building?

Our programs include targeted exercises designed to improve muscle strength and endurance, promoting overall physical health.

Why is flexibility training important?

Flexibility training helps enhance range of motion, prevent injuries, and improve overall body function and mobility.

Can exercise therapy help prevent injuries?

Yes, we create exercise plans that focus on proper movement techniques and strengthening key areas to reduce the risk of future injuries
