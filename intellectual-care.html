Frequently Asked Questions

What services do you offer for individuals with intellectual disabilities?

We provide individualised support, skill development programs, behavioural support, and assistance with daily living.

How do you promote skill development?

We design engaging programs tailored to enhance communication, social skills, and daily living skills to promote independence.

What kind of behavioural support do you provide?

Our behavioural support focuses on positive reinforcement and strategies to manage challenges, fostering a supportive environment.

How do you assist with social integration?

We create opportunities for social interaction and community engagement, helping individuals build relationships and feel included.
